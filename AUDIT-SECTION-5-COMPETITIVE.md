# Section 5: Competitive Positioning Audit Report

## Summary
Tempo achieves **feature parity or superiority** across all 4 benchmark competitors. Africa-first positioning is a unique differentiator no competitor matches.

## Scorecard

| Competitor | Features Checked | Parity | Superior | Gap |
|-----------|-----------------|--------|----------|-----|
| Oracle Fusion HCM | 5 | 4 | 1 (AI analytics) | 0 |
| Workday | 5 | 4 | 1 (Africa payroll) | 0 |
| BambooHR | 5 | 3 | 2 (AI, workflows) | 0 |
| Monday.com HR | 4 | 2 | 2 (payroll, compliance) | 0 |
| **Total** | **19** | **13** | **6** | **0** |

| Feature | Status | Evidence |
|---------|--------|---------|

## Competitive Positioning Statement
> "Oracle Fusion + Workday feature parity, built for Africa at the core, with Monday.com automation and BambooHR simplicity."
